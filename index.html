What is UiPath Uncharted?

Built for practitioners who want unfiltered UiPath stories.

Every episode is a deep conversation.

We spend 45 minutes on a guest's story and one specific product in the ecosystem: how it works, when to use it, and what nobody tells you.

Real guests

Why be a guest?

Your story deserves a platform.

📡

Reach the right audience

The UiPath community is global and highly engaged. Your story reaches practitioners, architects, and decision-makers.

⭐

Build your personal brand

Being featured as a subject matter expert is a credibility signal that lives on LinkedIn, in search, and across the community.

🤝

Contribute to the community

Your experience helps someone else avoid a mistake, make a better decision, or take a first step.

💡

Share something that matters

Most of your best work goes unseen. This is a structured, low-effort way to document it - 45 minutes of conversation, zero slides required.

What we ask of you

Clear expectations, low effort, high impact.

Requirements

  • Real-world experience with process automation
  • 45 minutes of your time via Zoom or Teams
  • A story worth telling - success, failure, or both
  • Willingness to share unfiltered opinions
  • No slides, no prep deck needed from your side

What you receive

  • Episode brief sent 5 days in advance
  • Full edited episode (yours to share)
  • 'I told my story' bragging rights
  • Written episode summary for your profile
  • Permanent feature in the season archive
